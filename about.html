About the Author

Owethu Sityata

Owethu is a dreamer, writer, and storyteller. Growing up in Khayelitsha- Cape Town, Owethu finds inspiration in personal experiences, childhood memories, and the many feelings that come with being human.

With a love for exploring the depths of life, Owethu creates stories that mix different styles, blending fiction, non-fiction, and surreal elements. This debut collection,"Beyond the Veil: Stories from My Life" is the start of Owethu's journey as a writer.
